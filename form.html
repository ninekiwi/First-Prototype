  Project Safety Checklist


1 Exterior of Project


1.1 Are all fences installed safely and supported properly?

1.4 All exterior equipment has proper flaggers, back-up signals, and is following speed limits.  



1.5 All trenching is being barricaded and workers are using shoring as required.  



1.5 All crane activities are working safely to ensure protection of workers and the public.  


1.7 Please indicate any safety issues specific to exterior of the project noted during the inspection. 





2. Personal Protective Equipment


2.1 Are all workers wearing hardhats, safety glasses, vests, and work boots? 



2.2 Any hazardous work occurring on site is utilizing all required PPE

2.5 Please indicate any safety issues specific to PPE noted during the inspection.





3. Housekeeping


3.1 Are all areas of the project being kept clean and free of debris?

3.3 Are any areas not up to housekeeping standards being addressed by the team? 



3.4 Project has adequate number of Fire Extinguishers and they are spaced as required. 



3.5 Please indicate any safety issues specific to housekeeping noted during the inspection.




4. Equipment On Site


4.1 All workers using equipment on site have been trained to use that specific equipment.  



4.2 All workers using equipment on site are following manufacturer's and OSHA's requirements for safe use.
